A game jam game made over the course of a week using Unity. I was a programmer in a team of 6.
In particular I worked on the player's movement abilities, as well as the trackers for the collectibles.
From this I learnt:
- Team building skills
- Working under the pressure of a tight deadline
- Working with WWise for Unity
